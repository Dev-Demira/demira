Process — How We Work Together

From first message
to launched site.

  1. 01

    Conversation

    We talk through your business, your customers, and what you actually need from a website. No pressure to buy more than necessary, just a clear picture of what will move your business forward.

  2. 02

    Plan & Deposit

    You receive a clear scope, timeline, and price before any work begins, so there are no surprise costs later. A fifty percent deposit secures your project slot and starts the build.

  3. 03

    Build & Refine

    The site gets built in stages, with review points at each major milestone. Adjustments happen at those checkpoints so the final result matches what you actually need, not a rushed first draft.

  4. 04

    Launch & Support

    The site goes live. You receive all files, hosting setup, and support after launch. If something needs a fix or a small change down the line, I remain reachable.
